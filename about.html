About Red Llama

The medical field can be rough, but your skin doesn't have to be! For the past 8 years I have been in the medical field working as an EMT and Surgical Technologist. I have always hated how dry my hands get from wearing gloves and constantly washing my hands all day. That's why I decided to make skin care products specifically formulated for health care workers. All of the products I make at Red Llama are made with all natural oils and butters, cosmetic grade fragrance and essential oils, and all natural colorants. All of our products are made with plant based materials, and are vegan friendly, kosher friendly, and cruelty free!
